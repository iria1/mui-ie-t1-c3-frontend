SCREEN TIME AND STRESS

This scatter plot shows how screen time can affect our mood and stress levels. When we spend too much time on screens, our brains can feel tired and stressed - the more red dots, the higher the stress! This visualization reminds us to take breaks and enjoy life offline too.

Mission: Every hour, do a 2-minute “screen shake” - dance or stretch!
